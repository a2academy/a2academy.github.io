Problem Solvers Math Circles

This online program trains students from 3-8 Grades towards prestigious math competitions such as Noetic, Math Kangaroo, Math Counts and American Mathematics Competitions (AMC) Series to become better Problem Solvers. Combine the best of the Eastern European Math Circles and US mathematical traditions.

Focus of the program is Not Just Math, but to make students better at problem solving

- Communicate Mathematically.
- Ability to Reason Well.
- Brainstorming together.
- Creative Puzzle Solving.
- Think Logically and Analytically.
- Modelling and Visualization
- Weekly homework and proctored exam in LMS.
- Make Connections among various math concepts.
